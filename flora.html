Ongoing project.
Flóra
I design and code a Icelandic an online feminist publication, Flóra, of which I am also on the editorial board. We have published three volumes since September 2018, as well as we run a blog, Flæði, where we publish content that does not fit the main publication. We have organised one event, which was on International Womens Day, where authors read from articles, poetry, discussed and played music.
